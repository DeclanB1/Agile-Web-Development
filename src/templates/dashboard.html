About SportsSync

We were tired of the constant back-and-forth trying to find a substitute player for our social sports teams, and it was frustrating to have to pay a forfeit fee when we couldn't find someone in time. SportsSync solves this problem effectively.

SportsSync is a web-based platform designed to make organizing and participating in social sports activities a breeze. Whether you are looking to join an existing event or create a new one, our platform helps you connect with fellow sports enthusiasts in your area.
