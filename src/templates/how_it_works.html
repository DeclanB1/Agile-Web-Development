Welcome to SportsSync!

SportsSync is your go-to platform for connecting with fellow sports enthusiasts in your community. Here’s a quick guide on how it works.

  1. Create an Account or Log In

    You can continue as a guest to explore events, but you’ll need an account to participate or create an event.

  2. Customize Your Profile

    Once logged in, add a bio, your sports preferences, and skill levels. This helps in tailoring your experience and connecting you with compatible players and events.

    3. Looking to join a sports event?
  3. Browse Events

    Use the “Browse Events" navigation tab to browse through ongoing sports events or matches. You can search based on sport, playing level, suburb, time, and location. This makes it easy to find exactly what you’re looking for or discover something new!

    4. Looking to find people for YOUR sports event?
  4. Post an Event

    Go to the “Post an Event" navigation tab to set up a new event. Provide details like the event title, sport type, playing level, number of players needed, time, and location. Once published, other users can find and join your event.

    5. Managing your events
  5. Manage Your Events under My Profile

    Check your “My Profile" page to view and manage the events you’ve posted or joined. You can edit details, communicate with participants, or cancel events if necessary.

Ready to Play?

SportSync is designed to make sports more accessible and enjoyable for everyone. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned athlete, our platform connects you to the sports you love. Start exploring today and bring your game to the next level!
