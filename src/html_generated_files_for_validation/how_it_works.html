How SportsSync Works!

Connect, play, and enjoy sports in your community with ease.

Customize Your Profile

Once logged in, add a bio, your sports preferences, and skill levels...

Browse Events

Use the “Browse Events" navigation tab to browse through ongoing sports events or matches. You can search based on sport, playing level, suburb, time, and location. This makes it easy to find exactly what you’re looking for or discover something new!

Post an Event

Go to the “Post an Event" navigation tab to set up a new event. Provide details like the event title, sport type, playing level, number of players needed, time, and location. Once published, other users can find and join your event.

Manage Your Events under My Profile

Check your “My Profile" page to view and manage the events you’ve posted or joined. You can edit details, communicate with participants, or cancel events if necessary.
